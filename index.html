Food Noise: What is it and why is it blowing up?

What is Food Noise?

Food noise refers to the constant, intrusive thoughts about food that can disrupt daily life and make it challenging to maintain healthy eating habits.

Why is it Popular?

The concept of food noise is gaining attention due to the rise in awareness about mental health and eating habits.
